Une Lueur d'espoir

(a glimmer of hope)

By: Margaux Jesse Pineda

The day welcomes you,
With a bliss of yellow hue.
Morning coffee goes brewing,
Life seems to be smooth sailing!

Highways are traffic and busy,
Car horns and daily hustles in the city!
It was all the ordinary,
Until the 22nd of January.

Dawn to dusk became unbelievably different,
Gloominess and sadness were undeniably apparent.
Amidst nature and its beauty’s force,
The world relentlessly changed its course.

At six feet apart we stand,
Face masks, shields, and sanitizers in demand!
With a hushed and distant space,
Each one of us shall be safe.

‘Tis our minds and souls,
Atomic thoughts are trapped in a hole.
One and all shall ought for the day
where tears turn into smiles,
Thus, sadness turns into great everlasting happiness.
